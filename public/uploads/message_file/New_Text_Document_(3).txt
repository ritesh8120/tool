Eh Dost ….

Ek waqt tha jab hum sath hua karte the
Phir ek umar ayi jab ankhon mai sapne liye chala karte the
Aaj ek raah par kadam ladkhadaye
Aas paas to koi nahi tha, dekha peeche mud kar to
Eh dost tu bhi na nazar aaye…

Zindagi mai kabhi-2 kuch baaton se pareshan ho jate the
Phir milkar ek dusre ko samjhate the…
Aaj bhi wo pal yaadein ban kar dil mai reh jaati hai
Eh dost teri bhaut yaad aati hai…

Jab bhi ghar par choti-2 baaton se ladkar tere paas aa jate the
Tere ghar ke darwaaze ki sidi par hi baidh jaate the,
Wahi baithkar tuhje dil ki har baat keh jaate the..
Dost aaj bhi wo baatein choti hi hoti hain,
Par koi samajhnewala nahi, isliye yeh aankhein roti hai..

Aaj bhi jab dekhte hain us jagah ko to,
Aaj bhi apni parchai wahan mujhe dikhti hai
Par tujhe jab dhoondte hain to tasveer dhoondli si kyon lagti hai..
Ab apni khamoshi bhi kuch keh jaati hai
Eh dost teri bhaut yaad aati hai…

Aaj dekh zindagi ka manzar kuch aur kehta hai
Aankhein wahi hai, aankhon mein sapne wahi hain
Par tabdilli zindagi ki tasveer badal deti hai
Un palon ko yaad kar yeh aankh aaj bhi muskurati hai
Eh dost teri bhaut yaad aati hai…

Kuch dost aise hote hain jinhe yaadein nahi bhool pati hain
Wo dost anmol hote hain, jab dil se dosti ki jaati hai..
Aaj bhi dekhte hain doston ko sath rehte hue
Kabhi gale milte, kabhi ladte jhagadte hue
In doston ko saath dekh, aankhein meri bhar jaati hai
Eh dost teri bhaut yaad aati hai…

apne dilo ke bich bharose ka dhaaga bandhate hai,
prem se, har mushkil suljhate hai,
wahi to dost kehlate hai...

duriyaa to sabhi ki zindagi me aati hai,
jo dur hokar bhi dilo ke pass reh jate hai,
aur yaado hi yaado me ek duje se baate banate hai,
wahi to dost kehlate hai...

apne aap par aur uspar hamesha bharosa rakhna,
kyoki do bichde hue bhi bharose se mil jaate hai,
aur jo isi bharose se apni dosti ko nibhate hai,
aaree yaaro, wahi to dost kehlate hai.
